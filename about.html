Who?

Gordon Ian Campbell. Scotsman, from a tiny town called Gourock.

Why?

I love creating things. Pictures, words, sounds. Anything, really. My love of all things computer started when I was nine years old.

What?

I've had my own design company, ZeeDesigns, since the year 2000. I started as a Flash animator and then self taught Actionscript 3.0. I went on to attain My Advanced Diploma in Graphic Design and Interactive Multimedia. You are welcome to see my journey - my resume is linked below.
